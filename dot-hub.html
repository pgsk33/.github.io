Dot Hub.

Unlock the power of your Glyph Matrix.

Dot Hub is the essential app for Nothing Phone owners who want to do more with their lights. We turn your Phone (3) or Phone (4a) Pro rear LED interface into a suite of interactive tools, visualization, and customizable displays.

Explore the Glyph Toys

From essential utilities to pure fun, Dot Hub bundles exclusive features designed specifically for the Glyph hardware.

Music Visualizer

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

Watch your music come to life. Your back lights react dynamically to every beat, turning your phone into a visual show. Pro version unlocks deeper personalization.

Ultimate AOD

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

The definitive Always-On Display for Glyph. Check time, call status, notifications, and dynamic info at a glance across over 20 unique animation styles that adapt to your activity.

Glyph Dice

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

Need a roll for your board game? Just tilt your phone to cast digital dice—from standard 6-sided to RPG variants—all visualized purely with your Glyph lights.

Coin Flip

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

Heads or tails? Can’t decide? Use the Glyph Coin Flip for a fast, random toss simulation activated by tilting your phone.

Glyph Compass

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

Find your way with a simple, futuristic navigation aid visualized through the LED matrix, responsive to how you hold your device.

Glyph Counter

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

Keep tally easily. A quick tilt of your phone increments the count on your Glyph interface—perfect for keeping track of anything without looking at the screen.

Breathing Tool

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro

Find your calm. Follow guided relaxation patterns shown through soothing light sequences directly on the Glyph matrix.

Matrix Timer / Tea Time

Phone (3) Phone (4a) Pro partially compatible

Perfect count-downs visualized on your lights. Can be integrated with other toys.
